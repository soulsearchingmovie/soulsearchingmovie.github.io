Soul Searching

Six feet Under, but it’s still not Over.

  • About
    The
    Film

  • Genre:

    Supernatural Detective Comedy/ Comedy Horror Language:​ Khmer with English Subtitle

  • Duration:

    100 minutes

  • Location:

    Phnom Penh, Cambodia

  • Status:

    Pre-production

    The idea of the story was first brought up by Kimsour Ly and was developed together in late 2017 by Kimsour Ly, Sokyou Chea and Dara Lim, as a teamwork screenplay workshop assignment. The screenplay was finally written and put together by Sokyou Chea in early 2018.

  • Story:

    Kimsour Ly, Sokyou Chea, Dara Lim

  • Writer & Director:

    Sokyou Chea

  • Producer:

    Rithea Pichith

  • Logline:

    A psychic man struggles to help an amnesiac ghost to uncover the mystery behind her death or else he will be haunted by her forever.
